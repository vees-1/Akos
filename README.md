## Why "Akos"?

The name **Akos** was chosen to reflect both the technical sophistication of our architecture and the human-centric goal of our mission.

* **Etymological Roots:** In Ancient Greek, **ἄκος (ákos)** translates to **"remedy," "cure," or "healing"**. [span_0](start_span)[span_1](start_span)This serves as the foundation of our mission: providing a fundamental medical remedy to underserved regions[span_0](end_span)[span_1](end_span).
* **Falcon-Like Precision:** In Hungarian, Akos means **"Falcon,"** symbolizing the keen vision and speed required for our image-based diagnostic AI.
* **Resilience in the Field:** In Ghanaian, the name represents **resilience and patience**, reflecting our "Offline-First" design that ensures life-saving triage continues even when the digital grid fails.
